# LoRaWAN Nucleo Pack with STM32L073RZ and SX1272

(info below is "label - arduino pin - stm32 pin")

SX1272 radio:

* DIO0 - Digital 2 - PA10
* DIO1 - Digital 3 - PB3
* DIO2 - Digital 4 - PB5
* DIO3 - Digital 5 - PB4
* NSS - Digital 10 - PB6
* MOSI - Digital 11 - PA7
* MISO - Digital 12 - PA6
* SCK - Digital 13 - PA5
* RESET - Analog 0 - PA0

Grove UART:

* UART RX - Digital 0 - PA3
* UART TX - Digital 1 - PA2

Grove I2C:

* I2C SCL - Digital 15 - PB8
* I2C SDA - Digital 14 - PB9

Grove ANA A1:

* AD1 - Analog 1 - PA1
* AD2 - Analog 2 - PA4

Grove ANA A3:

* AD3 - Analog 3 - PB0
* AD4 - Analog 4 - PC1

Grove DIO D6:

* D6 - Digital 6 - PB10
* D7 - Digital 7 - PA8

Grove DIO D8:

* D8 - Digital 8 - PA9
* D9 - Digital 9 - PC7
